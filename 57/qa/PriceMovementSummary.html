1. Price movement rules and/regulations.

1.1. Minimum price increments:

1.1.1. $0.01 for prices $1+,

1.1.2. $0.0001 for prices below $1

1.2. Trading Center Access Fees Limit

1.2.1. $0.003/share

1.3. LULD

1.3.1. Price Movement / Bands

  1. above $3
    1. Tier1
      1. reference price 5% (10% open/close)
    2. Tier2
      1. Ref Price 10% (20% open/close)
  2. $0.75 - $3
    1. 20% (40% open/close)
  3. below $0.75
    1. less of (\(0.15 or 75%) (\).30 or 75% at the open /close)

1.3.2. Reference Price

  1. 5 minute moving average is recalculated every 30 seconds.
  2. updated if new reference price is at least 1% higher or lower than the old one.

1.3.3. Halt Durations

  1. Limit State 15 Seconds
  2. LULD Halt 5 minutes

1.4. Circuit Breakers

1.4.1. Price Movements

  1. Levels
    1. 7% drop in S&P index => Level 1
    2. 13% drop in S&P index => Level 2
    3. 20% drop in S&P index => Level 3
  2. if before 3:25 pm
    1. Level 1 or Level 2
      1. halts market 15 mins
    2. Level 3
      1. Close market rest of the day
  3. If after 3:25 PM
    1. Level 1, 2 and 3:
    2. Close market rest of the day

1.5. Cleanrly Errorneous Numerical Guidelines

1.5.1. Listed Equities

  1. above $0.00 - including $25
    1. Normal Market Hours : 10%
    2. Outside Market Hours : 20%
  2. above $25 - including $50
    1. Normal Market Hours : 5%
    2. Outside Market Hours : 10%
  3. above $50
    1. Normal Market Hours : 3%
    2. Outside Market Hours : 6%

1.5.2. OTC

  1. $0.9999 and under
    1. 20%
  2. $1–$4.9999
    1. Low end of band: 20%
    2. High end of band: 10%
  3. $5–$74.9999
    1. 10%
  4. $75–$199.9999
    1. Low end of band: 10%
    2. High end of band: 5%
  5. $200–$499.9999
    1. 5%
  6. $500–$999.9999
    1. Low end of band: 5%
    2. High end of band: 3%
